## Overview
This project is based on the Tata Forage job simulation. The goal of the project was to analyze online retail transaction data and create business-focused visualisations for senior leadership (CEO and CMO).

## Business Objective
- Analyse revenue trends and seasonality
- Identify top revenue-generating countries and customers
- Understand product demand across regions
- Support business expansion and marketing decisions using data

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- Power Query (data cleaning)
- DAX (basic calculations)
- Microsoft Excel

## Business Questions & Visuals

**Q1. Monthly Revenue Trend (2011)**  
Analysed monthly revenue data to identify seasonal patterns and fluctuations that can support forecasting.

**Q2. Top 10 Countries by Revenue and Quantity (Excluding UK)**  
Identified countries contributing the highest revenue and sales volume to highlight expansion opportunities.

**Q3. Top 10 Customers by Revenue**  
Highlighted the highest-value customers to support customer retention and targeted marketing strategies.

**Q4. Demand by Region**  
Visualised product demand across regions using a map to help leadership prioritise high-demand markets.

## Deliverables
- Power BI report file (.pbix)
- Screenshots for each business question
- Clean, structured visuals aligned with executive requirements

## Outcome
The project delivers clear insights into revenue drivers, customer concentration, and regional demand, enabling data-driven decision-making for business growth.
